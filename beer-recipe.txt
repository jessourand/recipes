Ingredients:
  - 2 lbs Barley
  - 1 Gallon Water
  - 2 oz Hops
  - 1 pack ale yeast

Directions:
    1. Heat water to 150 degrees.
    2. Add barley to that shit. (1 hour)
    3. Sift barley out, and boil that wort for an hour. It's now called wort.
    4. Add hops whenever you feel like it.
    5. Cool that jank off. Then add the yeast.
    6. Plug it up and wait 2 weeks.
    7. Drink that shit.
